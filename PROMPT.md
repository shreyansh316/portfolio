> **Design Philosophy & Aesthetic Guidelines:**
> * **Layout Strategy:** Utilize a **"Bento Box" grid system** for project cards, metrics, and skills. Information should be compartmentalized into sleek, rounded-corner modules (e.g., 24px border radii) that fit together like a puzzle.
> * **Visual Style (Spatial UI):** Heavily feature layered glassmorphism (translucent backgrounds with deep background blurs), subtle inner borders (1px solid white at 10% opacity) to catch the light, and dynamic, slow-moving **mesh gradients** in the background.
> * **Typography:** Swiss design principles. Use highly legible geometric sans-serif fonts (like Inter, SF Pro, or Manrope). Create extreme contrast between massive, bold headings and muted, highly readable secondary text.
> * **Motion & Physics:** No linear animations. Use **spring physics** (via Framer Motion or GSAP) for all interactions. Hover states should feel tactile—cards should scale up slightly, icons should bounce into place, and color transitions must be buttery smooth.
> * **Color Palette:** Support a flawless Light/Dark mode toggle. Use monochromatic base layers (pure whites/grays or deep OLED blacks) punctuated by vibrant, highly saturated accent colors (like electric blue or neon violet) for buttons and active states.
